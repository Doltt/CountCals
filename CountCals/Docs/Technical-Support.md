## 2. Frequently Asked Questions

### 2.1 Camera and food recognition

- **The camera does not open or "Scan food" does nothing**  
  Make sure you have granted the App **Camera** access in **Settings → countCals → Camera**. If you previously denied access, turn it on there and try again.

- **Recognition results seem wrong or missing**  
  Food recognition runs on your device and works best with clear, well-lit photos of a single dish or item. Try centering the food, avoiding heavy shadows, and avoiding multiple mixed dishes in one shot.

- **Photos are not saved in the App**  
  By design, the App does not store your food photos. They are used only for on-device recognition and then discarded. Your privacy policy describes this in more detail.

### 2.2 Data and sync

- **My food log or settings disappeared**  
  All data is stored only on your device (no account, no cloud sync). If you reinstall the App, restore from a different device, or clear app data, that local data may be lost. We cannot recover it from our side.

- **I want to delete my data**  
  You can delete individual food entries from the daily food log in the App. To remove all data, uninstall the App; the system will remove the App and its local data.

### 2.3 Goals, activity level, and TDEE

- **How are my daily calorie goals calculated?**  
  The App uses your body data (e.g. height, weight) and activity level to estimate your daily energy needs (TDEE) and sets goals accordingly. You can change your activity level from the home screen (e.g. by double-tapping the calorie ring area, depending on the version).

- **I changed my weight or activity level but goals did not update**  
  After changing body data or activity level in Settings (or the relevant screen), return to the home/dashboard so the App can refresh. If it still does not update, try fully closing and reopening the App.

### 2.4 Widgets and Live Activity

- **Widget or Live Activity does not show or is outdated**  
  Widgets and Live Activity read from the same local data as the App. Make sure the App has been opened at least once so data exists. If the widget still does not update, try removing and re-adding the widget, or restarting your device.

- **I don’t want the widget / Live Activity**  
  You can remove the widget from your home screen like any other widget. Live Activity can be disabled in the App’s settings if that option is available in your version.

### 2.5 Accessibility and appearance

- **I use VoiceOver or Reduce Motion**  
  The App adds accessibility labels and respects system settings such as **Reduce Motion**. If you notice a screen or control that is not announced or behaves poorly with accessibility features, please tell us (see Contact).

- **Theme (light/dark) does not match my system**  
  Check **Settings → countCals** (or in-app Settings) for appearance options. The App can follow the system theme or use a fixed light/dark mode depending on your selection.

### 2.6 Crashes and performance

- **The App crashes or freezes**  
  Ensure you are on a supported iOS version (see **App information** below). Try closing the App from the app switcher and reopening it. If the issue continues, a reinstall may help (note: local data will be lost unless you have a device backup that includes app data). Please contact us with your device model and iOS version so we can investigate.

- **Recognition or the App feels slow**  
  Heavier use of on-device AI may temporarily use more battery and CPU. Closing other apps and ensuring sufficient free storage can help. If slowness is consistent, tell us your device model and we can consider optimizations.
